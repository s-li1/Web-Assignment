Background

I was born in Hong Kong before coming here to Australia when I was about 3 years old. My Dad is Hong Kong and my Mum is Indonesian Chinese. I don't have any siblings and my relatives are either in Hong Kong or Indonesia. So it's only the three of us. I never learnt my monther tongue which makes me feel left out sometimes when I do go overseas to meet my relatives. Since then I've been really determined to practice my Mandarin! I think it'll really help me in the future if I were to meet my extended family again, make friends or in business.

My HSC

I completed the HSC in 2019. I studied Business Studies, Chinese Continuers, Software Design and Development, Maths Advanced and English Advanced. I was quite happy with my results and overall I think it was a good year. My favourite subjects were actually Business Studies and Chinese! I initially wanted to study Business or Commerce but at the end chose Information Technology. Nonethless, I hope that I can continue my interests in those subjects whilst studying IT!

Computers

I would say I have a basic level of computing experience. I can fix most of the computer issues my parents have and I've studied IT subjects in highschool such as IST and SDD. I've also played around with some projects before related to web development and games. But there's still a lot to learn!
